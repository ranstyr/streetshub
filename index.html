About

Streets Hub is a non-profit organization which focuses in binding Rehovot, the ‘city of science and culture, to the ‘Start-Up nation, Israel’s economic growth engine

This vision brought up the idea to establish Rehovot as a technology hub in Israel’s ‘Shfela’ region by bringing together prominent Israel and Rehovot’s investors, entrepreneurs and scientists from “Weizmann institute of Science”, which was selected as one of the 10 top research institutions and the only one outside of the US.

We established Streets Hub in collaboration with experience entrepreneurs, investors, young specialists and talented scientists. We welcome those who share the same vision and aspiration to create innovative technologies to join/contact us and become part of the community
